Understanding Eternal and Plural Marriage

A short history on the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints' doctrine on Eternal Marriage and Plural Marriage.

This website will allow you to understand the details and history of both plural and eternal marriage and give you some other tools where you can continue to learn more of these topics. Feel free to ask questions in the comments on the topics you are interested in, and help others understand the concepts of eternal and plural marriage in relation to The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. (Doctrine and Covenants 132)
